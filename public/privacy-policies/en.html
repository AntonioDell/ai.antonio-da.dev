Privacy Policy

1. Identity and Contact Details

Antonio Dell'Annunziata is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us:

contact@antonio-da.dev

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We collect your email address when you voluntarily sign up for our newsletter. We use your email address to send you our newsletter and to inform you about our latest content, promotions and updates. We do not use your email address for any other purposes.

3. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing your email address is your consent, which you provide when you sign up for the newsletter.

4. Recipients of Personal Data

We use SendGrid as our email service provider to send newsletters. By signing up for our newsletter, you acknowledge that your email address will be shared with SendGrid for this purpose. SendGrid is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable privacy laws. For more information on SendGrid's privacy practices, please see their privacy policy at: https://www.twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy#data-about-our-customers-end-users.

5. Data Retention Period

We will retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions in each newsletter. After unsubscribing, we will delete your email address from our records.

6. Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Information: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we have stored about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data we have stored about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You have the right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided above.

7. Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your email address at any time. To do so, you can unsubscribe from our newsletter by following the instructions in each email. After unsubscribing, we will delete your email address from our records.

8. Right to Lodge a Complaint with a Supervisory Authority

If you believe that your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your country.

10. Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling with your personal data.
